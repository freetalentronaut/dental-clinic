Dental Information

Quick, trustworthy notes to help you make informed decisions.

How often should I get scaling (cleaning)? ⌄

Most people benefit from scaling every 6 months. If you have gum concerns, your dentist may suggest a 3–4 month interval.

Do fillings hurt? ⌄

Modern anaesthesia makes fillings comfortable. We also shape and polish carefully, so your bite feels natural immediately.

Root Canal vs. Extraction ⌄

We preserve natural teeth where possible (RCT). Extraction is chosen when prognosis is poor or infection is extensive.

Are implants right for me? ⌄

Implants offer excellent function and aesthetics. We assess bone support, health, and habits before recommending them.
